    WHAT IT DOES
    This clear, fragrance-free, non-greasy cleansing balm leaves your skin feeling fresh and primed for the rest of your routine.

    Skin Type & Concerns
    Skin Type
    • All Skin Types
    Skin Concerns

    How to Use
    1. Use as first step in routine. Apply to dry skin.
    2. Massage balm in circular motions across face with fingertips, including over closed eyes for eye makeup removal.
    3. Rinse with water or damp cloth. Can be used morning or night.
